Build Your Case.

Your case is your story. It is everything you have done to get your housing issues resolved, such as capturing photo evidence and notifying your landlord - all the way up reporting code violations and going to Housing Court. Your case helps you avoid disputes by providing a clear record of communication, but if things get more serious, it can also be used as a powerful tool in legal proceedings. JustFix.nyc is here to make the process of building your case as easy as possible.

how it works

Inspect

Start with a room-to-room inspection of all the issues that you might be currently experiencing in your home.

document

Document

All of your evidence - things like photo evidence, text messages, phone calls, complaint tracking numbers, and more are securely stored in one place.

report

Report

Based on your neighborhood, type of housing, and issues with your home, we craft a personalized plan-of-attack for you to get things fixed.

share

Share

As you continue to build your case, you can easily share it with advocates, lawyers, and even judges as a way to tell your story and get the results you deserve.
